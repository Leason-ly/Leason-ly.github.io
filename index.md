## About Me

I am a PhD student in the [College of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence](https://ai.fudan.edu.cn/) at [Fudan University](https://www.fudan.edu.cn/en/), under the supervision of Prof. [Bihuan Chen](https://chenbihuan.github.io/) and Prof. [Xin Peng](https://cspengxin.github.io/), mentored by Prof. Bihuan Chen. I received my bachelor's degree in software engineering from [Soochow University](http://eng.suda.edu.cn/) in 2022.

## Research Interests

- **AI system engineering:** SE4AI, AI4SE, Fuzzing, etc.
- I am particularly interested in LLM-based agents, including their planning, tool use, memory management, runtime monitoring, and safety assurance. I also study the infrastructure that supports AI agents, such as execution harnesses, sandbox environments, evaluation frameworks, and runtime guardrails. My research also explores the testing and safety assurance of autonomous driving systems. I focus on simulation-based testing, scenario generation, dynamic traffic participant behavior modeling, and runtime safety monitoring for autonomous driving systems.
