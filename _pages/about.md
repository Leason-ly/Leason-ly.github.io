About Me
------

👔 I am a PhD student in [College of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence](https://ai.fudan.edu.cn/), [Fudan University](https://www.fudan.edu.cn/en/) under the supervision of Prof. [Bihuan Chen](https://chenbihuan.github.io/) and Prof. [Xin Peng](https://cspengxin.github.io/), mentored by Prof. Bihuan Chen. I received my bachelor degree in software engineering from [Soochow University](http://eng.suda.edu.cn/) in 2022.

🌱 I currently major in software engineering and my research interests AI systems engineering, automatic driving system and simulation testing.

Research Interests
======

🤖 AI System Engineering
------
My research focuses on building reliable, safe, and efficient AI-driven software systems. I am particularly interested in LLM-based agents, including their planning, tool use, memory management, runtime monitoring, and safety assurance. I also study the infrastructure that supports AI agents, such as execution harnesses, sandbox environments, evaluation frameworks, and runtime guardrails. My goal is to improve the reliability and controllability of AI systems when they interact with external tools, execute complex workflows, and operate in open-ended environments.

🚗 Automatic Driving System and Simulation Testing
------
My research also explores the testing and safety assurance of autonomous driving systems. I focus on simulation-based testing, scenario generation, dynamic traffic participant behavior modeling, and runtime safety monitoring for autonomous driving systems. In particular, I am interested in identifying safety-critical scenarios, improving the realism and efficiency of simulation testing, and designing assurance mechanisms that help autonomous driving systems behave more safely and robustly in complex driving environments.
