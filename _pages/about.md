## About Me

I am a PhD student in the [School of Computer Science and Technology](https://cs.fudan.edu.cn/) at [Fudan University](https://www.fudan.edu.cn/en/), advised by Prof. [Bihuan Chen](https://chenbihuan.github.io/) and Prof. [Xin Peng](https://cspengxin.github.io/). I received my bachelor's degree in software engineering from [Soochow University](http://eng.suda.edu.cn/) in 2022.

## Research Interests

* **AI system engineering:** reliable execution, tool use, memory, runtime monitoring, and safety assurance for LLM-based agents.
* **Autonomous driving simulation testing:** scenario generation, dynamic traffic participant modeling, violation diagnosis, and runtime safety monitoring.
